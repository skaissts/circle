Sculptures

Material: wood (solid wood).

Technique: hand carving, sanding.

An organic abstract form with an elongated, almost musical silhouette. Flowing lines and asymmetry emphasize a sense of movement and inner tension within the figure. Traces of manual craftsmanship are intentionally preserved as part of the sculpture’s texture and expression. The small perforations in the slender element introduce rhythm and evoke associations with sound or breath.

The work explores the balance between mass and fragility, stillness and fluidity, leaving space for the viewer’s personal interpretation.

Material: stone / ceramic.

Technique: hand modeling, carving, surface texturing.

A figurative sculptural form reduced to an essential, almost archetypal presence. The elongated head and open mouth suggest a frozen cry, breath, or silent vocalization, capturing a moment of raw emotional tension. The rough, uneven surface emphasizes the material’s resistance and enhances the sense of vulnerability and erosion.

The separation between head and body subtly reinforces the feeling of inner rupture, while the compact, grounded mass contrasts with the expressive intensity of the face. The work explores themes of voice, confinement, and the physical embodiment of emotion.

Websites

Matcha Matcha — Website Design

A clean, minimal website designed for a matcha café in Kassel. The visual concept is built around calmness, clarity, and product focus, using a natural color palette, generous spacing, and simple typography. The layout highlights the brand atmosphere while keeping the user experience fast and intuitive.

Functions & Features

  • Fully responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Clear navigation with logical page structure
  • Smooth scrolling and subtle interactions
  • Strong visual hierarchy and CTA placement
  • Brand-consistent color system and typography
  • Optimized performance and fast loading
  • Easy access to location, menu, and social links

The website communicates the brand's identity efficiently while maintaining a modern, lightweight, and user-friendly interface suitable for both casual visitors and returning customers.

matchamatcha.site

Ilona's Nails Kulture — Website Design

A clean, service-focused one-page website designed for a premium nail salon in Vienna. The layout prioritizes clarity and ease of use, with a clear hierarchy of sections for services, pricing, portfolio images, and contact details. Visual simplicity and consistent typography support quick scanning, while high-quality imagery showcases the salon's work and reinforces trust.

Functions & Features

  • Multilingual interface (DE / RU / EN)
  • Service list with prices
  • About / philosophy section highlighting expertise
  • Portfolio gallery
  • Clear call-to-action — appointment booking via phone/WhatsApp
  • Contact details with address and direct links

The site efficiently communicates professionalism, highlights the salon's quality standards and services, and makes booking fast and intuitive for new and returning clients.

ilonasnailkultur-pbwk.vercel.app

Viva Ästhetik — Website Design & Functionality

A multi-page website created for a cosmetic clinic in Kassel, designed to present services clearly and support a structured, professional user journey. The design combines a modern, clinical aesthetic with approachable visuals and typography, reinforcing trust and quality. Each section is crafted to guide users from discovery to booking with minimum friction.

Functions & Features

  • Multi-page architecture for comprehensive service presentation
  • Integrated online booking / appointment system
  • Service detail pages with descriptions and pricing
  • Contact forms and request capture
  • Location and opening hours visibility
  • Smooth navigation and clear call-to-action across pages
  • Consistent branding across site structure

The site supports both informational depth and functional interaction, enabling users to explore treatments, learn about expertise, and directly schedule appointments — enhancing user engagement and business conversion.

Bio / CV

Founded on April 12, 2023, in Prague, the "Circle Studio" integrates both physical and digital art disciplines.

Our work spans fine art, sculpture, ceramics, graphic design, web design, and digital branding. We focus on combining artistic depth with modern technology, creating cohesive visual solutions across multiple mediums.

Annel Amanzholova

General Director · Creative Producer

Education:

Academy of Arts, Kassel, Germany — Fine Arts (Painting)

University of Vienna, Austria — Art History

Annel works at the intersection of contemporary art and creative direction. Her practice is rooted in classical painting and expanded through ceramics and mixed media. She confidently operates across multiple artistic techniques, combining material experimentation with conceptual clarity. As a creative producer, she oversees artistic vision, visual narratives, and the overall aesthetic direction of the studio.

Team & Workflow

The studio operates with a flexible outsourcing model. For each project, specialists are selected according to the task — including developers, 3D artists, motion designers, illustrators, photographers, and copywriters. This structure allows us to assemble a precise, task-oriented team for every project while maintaining high quality and efficiency.
